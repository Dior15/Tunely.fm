About Tunely.fm

Tunely.fm is an open-source website dedicated to providing free, streamable music for anyone. We boast a small library of accessible tunes that you can play at your leisure.

Our stats page will show you your music listening metrics. Check it out, maybe you'll learn something about yourself.

We encourage you to bookmark this page and return to it later when you want to relax with some songs. Studying, dishwashing, driving; if you have internet, come to Tunely.fm.
